branding definition

The company is a online shopping company that sells modern chairs of different styles such as office, home, decoration etcs. By focusing on one paticular product, company aims to provide broader range of choices for consumers to choose.
The potential customers of the company various. It can be anyone that needs modern styled chair with decent price and quality. But mainly,we are targeting towards people from age 20 - 55 who need work in office, need chair for meeting or at home.
The company is focusing on a very specified product. However, with in this type, we provide tons of options for customers to choose and compare, not just in general.Also, there are always promotions and discount as well as seasonal activities, many great deals and nice prices are offered to our customers. Lastly, we have fantastic delivery service that customer can choose a quicker delivery speed with low price and they are able to track their product as well.
