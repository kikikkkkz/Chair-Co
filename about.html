About Chair&Co

We are an online chair company that offers modern chairs of various types and materials. Our rich selection of chairs will provide more options for you to choose. Our Business

Chair&Co is a new company founded in 2008. We established as an online company to provide exclusive deals of modern chairs internationally. Each year, we reach out to talented designers for our new series. We build a connection with the local design community as well.
